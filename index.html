Word Search Generator

How to Play

Word Search is a puzzle game where you find hidden words in a grid of letters.

Game Rules:

  1. Enter Words: Type comma-separated words in the input field.
  2. Select Difficulty: Choose Easy, Medium, or Hard mode.
  3. Generate: Click the Generate button to create your puzzle.
  4. Find Words: Words can be placed horizontally, vertically, and diagonally (more directions in harder modes).
  5. Select Words: Click and drag from the first letter to the last letter of a word to select it.
  6. Use Hints: Click the Hint button if you're stuck (costs 10 points).

Scoring System:

  • Points are based on word length and difficulty level.
  • Finding consecutive words builds a streak multiplier for bonus points!
  • Completing the puzzle quickly earns you a time bonus.
  • Using hints costs 10 points each.

Tips:

  • Look for word patterns in all directions.
  • Start with longer words as they're easier to spot.
  • Save hints for when you're really stuck.
  • Try to maintain your streak for maximum points!
